Tax rates & thresholds applied

  • Personal allowance: £12,570 (tapers above £100,000)
  • Basic rate 20%: £12,571 – £50,270
  • Higher rate 40%: £50,271 – £125,140
  • Additional rate 45%: above £125,140
  • NI 8%: earnings between £12,570 – £50,270
  • NI 2%: earnings above £50,270
  • Does not include student loan, pension, or salary sacrifice
  • Scotland has different income tax bands (not shown)
